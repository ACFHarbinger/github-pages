<h4>CSE Master of Science (MSc) Dissertation</h4>
<p>Towards the completion of my CSE MSc degree at <a href="https://tecnico.ulisboa.pt/en/">IST</a>, I worked as a Graduate Student Researcher at <a href="https://gaips.inesc-id.pt/">GAIPS</a>, where I wrote a Dissertation titled <a href="assets/docs/IST_UL___MEIC_Thesis___Dissertacao_final__Copy_.pdf">'Leveraging Deep Unsupervised Models Towards Learning Robust Multimodal Representations'</a>. For this Dissertation, I developed three new Multimodal Deep Unsupervised Models - which combined principles from <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/autoencoders.pdf">Denoising Auto-Encoders</a>, <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/geometric_multimodal_cl.pdf">Geometric Multimodal Contrastive Learning</a> and <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/ssl_video_ooo_nets.pdf">Odd-One-Out Networks</a> - and compared their robustness against missing modalities, noisy data and adversarial examples with other deep multimodal models such as the <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/multimodal_gen_models_scalable_wsl.pdf">MVAE</a>. The final document and the respective code are the following (I also wrote a paper, but I still want to improve it before posting it to ARXIV):</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="assets/docs/IST_UL___MEIC_Thesis___Dissertacao_final__Copy_.pdf">Dissertation</a></li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/ACFPeacekeeper/rgmc">Github Repository</a></li>
</ul>
<h4>WSmart Route+</h4>
<p>For the diploma in Methodologies and Techniques of Scientific Research, I'm currently working on the <a href="https://cegist.tecnico.ulisboa.pt/projects/wsmart-route-towards-smart-waste-collection-route-planning-system">WSmart Route+</a> project, which seeks to expand upon the research to develop a system for waste collection based on a real time route planning sytem made during the <a href="https://wsmartroute.tecnico.ulisboa.pt/#title">WSmart Route</a> project, which was funded by the <a href="https://www.fct.pt/en/">FCT</a> foundation and the <a href="https://mitportugal.org/">MIT Portugal</a> program and combines efforts from <a href="https://ersuc.pt/">Ersuc</a>, <a href="https://tecnico.ulisboa.pt/en/">IST</a>, the <a href="https://www.uc.pt/">University of Coimbra</a>, <a href="https://evox.pt/">EVOX Technologies</a> and the <a href="https://www.mit.edu/">Massachusetts Institute of Technology</a>. I am trying to develop new <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/intro_drl.pdf">Deep Reinforcement Learning</a> methods to solve <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/rl_co.pdf">Combinatorial Optimization</a> tasks, particularly the <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/solving_cvrp_demands_fuzzy_rv.pdf">Capacitated Vehicle Routing Problem (CVRP) with Fuzzy Demand</a> (which is an instance of the <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/drl_solving_het_cvrp.pdf">CVRP</a>) and  the <a href="assets/docs/literature/papers/orienteering.pdf">Orienteering Problem (OP)</a>. Here are some publications related to the project:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="assets/images/workshop_posters/workshop-poster.png">WSmart Route Workshop Poster</a></li>
