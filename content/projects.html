CSE Master of Science (MSc) Dissertation

Towards the completion of my CSE MSc degree at IST, I worked as a Graduate Student Researcher at GAIPS, where I wrote a Dissertation titled 'Leveraging Deep Unsupervised Models Towards Learning Robust Multimodal Representations'. For this Dissertation, I developed three new Multimodal Deep Unsupervised Models - which combined principles from Denoising Auto-Encoders, Geometric Multimodal Contrastive Learning and Odd-One-Out Networks - and compared their robustness against missing modalities, noisy data and adversarial examples with other deep multimodal models such as the MVAE. The final document and the respective code are the following (I also wrote a paper, but I still want to improve it before posting it to ARXIV):

WSmart Route+

For the diploma in Methodologies and Techniques of Scientific Research, I'm currently working on the WSmart Route+ project, which seeks to expand upon the research to develop a system for waste collection based on a real time route planning sytem made during the WSmart Route project, which was funded by the FCT foundation and the MIT Portugal program and combines efforts from Ersuc, IST, the University of Coimbra, EVOX Technologies and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I am trying to develop new Deep Reinforcement Learning methods to solve Combinatorial Optimization tasks, particularly the Capacitated Vehicle Routing Problem (CVRP) with Fuzzy Demand (which is an instance of the CVRP) and the Orienteering Problem (OP).
