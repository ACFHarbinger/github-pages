Current Work

I'm currently a First Stage Researcher at INESC-ID - where I'm developing new Deep Reinforcement Learning methods to solve Combinatorial Optimization tasks - and a invited Assistant Professor at the Computer Science and Engineering department of IST, where I teach courses about Distributed Systems, Cloud Computing and Virtualization and Computer Organization. I am also co-founding a Game Development studio called Retsam Entertainment with @jfperodrigues and @fafpinheiro.

Previous Work

Previously, I was a Computer Programmer Analyst at the Research & Development department of qubIT - where I developed a Java application to integrate several Git-based version control services, as well as a microservice and a set of cryptographic tools to allow clients to securely manage several keystore files spread across multiple servers - and a Graduate Student Researcher at GAIPS, where I developed new Multimodal Deep Learning architectures and frameworks with a focus towards increased robustness against noisy data and adversarial attacks.

Hobbies & Personal Interests

During my spare time, I enjoy reading books (including both fiction and non-Fiction), playing a wide variety of games (such as videogames, table top games, card games, etc.), listening to music and watching movies, anime and TV series. Beyond this, I also like to work on hardware and software for personal projects, which I find to be both inherently fulfilling and a complement to my theoretical understanding of the world.

Knowledge Areas of Interest

For as long as I can remember, I have been a naturally curious person which has sought to better understand the world by performing rudimentary experiments and learning as much knowledge as possible. Today, I continue my quest for knowledge, now not only due to an inherent curiosity, but also with the goal of using this knowledge and information to further my own research work. The main focus of my research is the development of new Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques to tackle real world problems. Towards this goal, I seek to not only learn about topics directly related to AI, such as Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL) and Reinforcement Learning (RL), but also about natural intelligence in general, particularly the interdisciplinary study of the mind known as cognitive science, which integrates several diverse fields such as AI, neuroscience, psychology, anthropology, philosophy, and linguistics. Overall, I am interested if the following fields of knowledge:

  • Computer Science and Engineering
    • Artificial Intelligence
      • Machine Learning
        • Reinforcement Learning
        • Deep Learning
        • Graph Machine Learning
        • Meta-Learning
        • Adversarial Attacks
      • Multimodal Learning
        • Computer Vision
        • Natural Language Processing
        • Audio Analysis & Processing

Academia

I initially pursued a Integrated Master's (BSc + MSc) in Computer Science at NOVA - FCT, and a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Biochemistry at UL - FCUL, both of which I did not complete. Afterwards, I studied for and completed a Bachelor of Science (BSc) and a Master of Science (MSc) degrees in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at UL - IST. During my BSc, I learned about core concepts in Computer Science, Mathematics, Computer Engineering and Physics (along with some minor courses about HASS and Management) and I also participated in a Athens Network exchange program at Mines Paris - PSL, where I learned about Extreme Value Statistics. During my MSc, I did a double major in Artificial Intelligence and Cyber-Security (and also did some courses about Game Development) and I participated in a semester exchange program at POSTECH, where I studied Machine Learning and Mathematics.

I'm currently taking a Advanced Formation Diploma in Methodologies and Techniques of Scientific Research at UL - IST.
